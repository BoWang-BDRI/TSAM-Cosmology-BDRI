# Trinity Space Arbitration Model (TSAM)
### Bipolar-Dynamics Research Institute (BDRI)

Welcome to the official repository of the **Trinity Space Arbitration Model (TSAM)**. This research proposes a novel cosmological framework based on a closed-loop universe topology to explain high-redshift galaxy anomalies observed by JWST.

## 核心理论概览 / Core Theory
TSAM replaces the expanding universe paradigm with a **Ternary Space Arbitration Model**, consisting of:
* **Anode & Cathode:** The dual poles of the universal cycle.
* **Tension Wall (The Brane):** A high-potential boundary where photons lose energy, manifesting as cosmological redshift ($z$).

## 解决的问题 / Problems Solved
* **JWST High-z Anomalies:** Explains why galaxies at $z > 13$ appear "impossible" under the $\Lambda$CDM model.
* **Cosmological Redshift:** Interprets redshift as topological energy loss rather than recession velocity.
